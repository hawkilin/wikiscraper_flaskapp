Welcome to my Wikipedia/Wiktionary Scraper App

If you want to scrape wikipedia, add /wikipedia?term=this_is_where_you_put_your_search_term to the sites URL

If you want to scrape wiktionary add /wiktionary?term=this_is_where_you_put_your_search_term to the sites URL

If your search term is more than one word, put underscores between the words.
